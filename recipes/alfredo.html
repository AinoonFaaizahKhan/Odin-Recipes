Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description:

Chicken Alfredo is a classic Italian-American comfort dish featuring tender seasoned chicken and pasta—most traditionally fettuccine—tossed in a rich, velvety sauce made of butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 200 g / 7oz chicken breast, cut in half horizontally
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 250 g / 8 oz fettuccine
  • 2 cups milk, any fat %
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken stock / broth
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 cup thickened / heavy cream (Note 1)
  • 3/4 cup freshly grated parmesan (Note 2)
  • Parsley, for garnish
